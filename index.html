About Our Teacher Panel

The Teacher Panel of the Department of Geography is composed of highly qualified, dedicated, and experienced educators who are committed to academic excellence and student success. Our teachers play a vital role in shaping studentsâ€™ knowledge, critical thinking abilities, and research skills in the field of geography.

Each member of our teacher panel specializes in diverse areas such as Physical Geography, Human Geography, Environmental Studies, GIS & Remote Sensing, Cartography, and Climate Studies.
Through interactive teaching methods, fieldwork guidance, laboratory supervision, and research mentoring, they ensure a balanced combination of theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Beyond classroom teaching, our teachers actively engage in academic research, seminars, workshops, and community-based studies. Their guidance helps students develop environmental awareness, analytical skills, and a global perspective. The Department takes pride in its teacher panel, whose continuous support and mentorship inspire students to achieve academic excellence and contribute meaningfully to society.
