About Our Teacher Panel

The Teacher Panel of the Department of Geography consists of qualified and experienced educators dedicated to academic excellence and student development. Our teachers specialize in areas such as Physical and Human Geography, Environmental Studies, GIS & Remote Sensing, Cartography, and Climate Studies. Through interactive teaching, fieldwork, and research guidance, they help students build strong theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and a global perspective.
